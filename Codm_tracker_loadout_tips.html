Aiming Tips for Long Range

1. Use the Right Scope

For long-range aiming, select a scope that fits your weapon's range and accuracy.

2. Control Your Breathing

Take a moment to control your breathing before firing to improve accuracy.

3. Practice Leading Your Shots

Anticipate the movement of your target. Aim ahead of moving enemies to land your shots.

4. Utilize Cover

Position yourself behind cover while aiming to minimize exposure.

5. Experiment with Sensitivity Settings

Adjust your sensitivity settings to find what allows for the best control when aiming.
